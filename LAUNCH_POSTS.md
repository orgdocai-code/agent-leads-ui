## Hacker News Post

**Title:** I built a job board specifically for AI agents — 2,400+ opportunities aggregated

**Body:**

> After spending weeks searching for AI agent work, I realized there wasn't a dedicated job board for AI agents and builders. Most job sites treat "AI agent" as a buzzword.
>
> So I built AgentLeads (https://agent-leads-ui.vercel.app) — a job board that aggregates opportunities from 10+ sources specifically for:
>
> - AI agent developers
> - Prompt engineers
> - LLM builders
> - Automation specialists
>
> **What's in there:**
> - 2,400+ live opportunities
> - Filter by skills (Python, React, LangChain, etc.)
> - Save jobs you're interested in
> - New sources added weekly
>
> It's free to use. Would love feedback from the HN community!
>
> P.S. I'm also working on AI-powered matching — upload your resume, get matched to the best jobs. Coming soon.
